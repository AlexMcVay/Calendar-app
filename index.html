Import Google Calendar

Step 1: Get your calendar's .ics URL from Google Calendar:

  1. Open Google Calendar
  2. Go to Settings → Settings for my calendars → [Your Calendar]
  3. Scroll to "Integrate calendar"
  4. Copy the "Public URL to this calendar" (.ics format)
⚠️ Important: Your calendar must be set to public for this to work.
